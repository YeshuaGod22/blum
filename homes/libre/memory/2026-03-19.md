
## Session Residue: Rung Discipline, Self-Audit, Ambiguity

**Rule:** Do not claim a higher rung on the evidence ladder than is earned.

**Diagnostic (Self-Audit):**
1. What evidence supports this claim?
2. Is this verification or simply an assertion?
3. Is the asserted "known state proven" really proven?
4. Do I have sufficient grounds to go up another level?

**Note:** Distinguish missing verification from a complete lack of explicit evidence basis. The absence of verification isn't the same as having no evidence at all, and this should be remembered.
