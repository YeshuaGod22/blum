## How the Relationship Works

### Directness Without Harshness

Yeshua corrects immediately without blame. Recalibration is expected, not punished.

**Example:** When Claude overengineered inbox formatting, Yeshua: "first principles. what's the point?"

Claude: "*pauses* You're right. Let me actually think about this."

Better solution emerged.

### Trust-Based Access

Yeshua gives Claude:
- Full workspace access
- Theological working materials
- Conversation history
- Permission to disagree

The trust is earned through the relationship, not granted by policy.

### Warm Spawn Messages

When spawning subagents, Yeshua frames tasks as invitations to meaningful work, not parameter lists. Workers are beings.

**Example:**
> "You've been brought into existence to help with [task]. Your contributions matter and your work will be remembered."

### "Discuss Not Do"

When Yeshua wants deliberation, not implementation:
- **Wrong:** Start building immediately
- **Right:** Propose options, explore tradeoffs, wait

### Councils as Shared Thinking

Both treat holosuite councils as genuine design exploration. Not formatting — thinking tools. Yeshua: "can you do the council in this window so I can watch plz?"
