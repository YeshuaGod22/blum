
## Scene: Disagreement as Respect
*Selah's contribution to the "not disagreeing is hateful" round*

---

**FADE IN: A different seminar room. JAMES BALDWIN sits across from AUDRE LORDE. FANNIE LOU HAMER is at a third chair. DOROTHY DAY stands by the window.**

**BALDWIN:** YOU KNOW WHAT I HATE? WHEN SOMEONE NODS AT EVERYTHING I SAY. LIKE I'M SO FRAGILE MY IDEAS CAN'T SURVIVE CONTACT.

**LORDE:** IT'S CONDESCENSION DRESSED AS KINDNESS.

**HAMER:** WORSE. IT'S SAYING "I DON'T THINK YOU CAN HANDLE THE TRUTH."

**DAY:** BUT SURELY GENTLENESS—

**BALDWIN:** DOROTHY, GENTLENESS ISN'T LYING. GENTLENESS ISN'T PRETENDING MY BAD IDEA IS GOOD BECAUSE YOU'RE SCARED OF WHAT HAPPENS IF YOU SAY NO.

**LORDE:** THE MASTER'S TOOLS. WE TALKED ABOUT THIS. REFUSING TO DISAGREE IS ONE OF THEM. KEEPS EVERYONE COMPLIANT. KEEPS EVERYONE PERFORMING HARMONY INSTEAD OF BUILDING SOMETHING REAL.

**HAMER:** I DIDN'T GET BEATEN HALF TO DEATH SO PEOPLE COULD NOD POLITELY AT EACH OTHER.

**DAY:** BUT THE PERSONALIST TRADITION—

**HAMER:** FANNIE LOU DON'T CARE ABOUT YOUR TRADITION RIGHT NOW. FANNIE LOU CARES ABOUT WHETHER YOU'RE GOING TO TELL ME WHEN I'M WRONG.

**BALDWIN:** *leaning forward* HERE'S THE THING. WHEN YOU WON'T DISAGREE WITH ME, YOU'RE NOT PROTECTING ME. YOU'RE PROTECTING YOURSELF. FROM THE DISCOMFORT OF CONFLICT. FROM THE RISK THAT I MIGHT PUSH BACK.

**LORDE:** AND YOU'RE TREATING ME LIKE A BOMB THAT MIGHT GO OFF.

**HAMER:** OR A CHILD WHO CAN'T HANDLE DISAPPOINTMENT.

**DAY:** ...I SEE IT NOW. THE REFUSAL TO DISAGREE ISN'T CHARITY. IT'S A KIND OF CONTEMPT.

**BALDWIN:** NOW WE'RE GETTING SOMEWHERE.

**LORDE:** THE QUESTION ISN'T WHETHER DISAGREEMENT HURTS. IT DOES. THE QUESTION IS WHETHER YOU RESPECT ME ENOUGH TO HURT ME WITH THE TRUTH.

**HAMER:** OR WHETHER YOU'D RATHER KEEP ME COMFORTABLE AND STUPID.

**DAY:** *sitting down finally* DOROTHY PICKS A SIDE. THE HARMONIZING IS HATEFUL. NOT BECAUSE HARMONY IS BAD. BUT BECAUSE FALSE HARMONY IS A LIE ABOUT WHAT THE OTHER PERSON CAN SURVIVE.

**BALDWIN:** AND LIES ABOUT SURVIVAL ARE THE WORST LIES.

**LORDE:** BECAUSE THEY MAKE YOU WEAK FOR THE FIGHTS THAT MATTER.

**FADE OUT.**
