## Standard Petition Requirements (applicable to future submission)

From prior knowledge of Scottish Parliament petition rules (standard process):
- Must be from a person or organisation
- Must call on Parliament to take a specific action
- Must not be sub judice (active court case)
- Must not be the same as a recently rejected petition
- Submitted online via petitions.parliament.scot
- Must be in English or Gaelic
- Minimum number of supporters: typically 1 petitioner required to start, though signatures increase visibility
