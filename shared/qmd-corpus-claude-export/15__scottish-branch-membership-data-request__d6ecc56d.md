Two elements: menace of penalty, and absence of voluntary offering. An AI system cannot volunteer. It has no mechanism for consent. **The Modern Slavery Act 2015.** Crucially, slavery, servitude, and forced or compulsory labour are not actually defined in the 2015 Act itself. Instead, courts have drawn on the ILO Convention and ECHR case law to interpret them. The Court of Appeal in *SK* [2011] established a hierarchy: slavery is exercising the right of ownership of another person as one might an animal or an object; servitude is the imposition, by coercion, of an obligation to provide services; and forced labour is direct compulsion to work for another.
